🗼Travel itinerary to Paris, France 🗼

This is a travel itinerary trip to Paris, France. Go to Tripadvisor for hepful information about places to visit.

Date: Day 1
Location: Paris, France
Activities:
  • Visit Eiffel Tower
  • Visit Louvre Museum
  • Cruise on the Seine River
Notes: Take awesome pictures from any of the floors of the Tower and get ready to see Paris from a different and spectacular perspective.
Date: Day 2
Location: Paris, France
Activities:
  • Visit Notre Dame Cathedral Paris
  • Visit Luxembourg Gardens
  • Visit The Arc de Triomphe
Notes: Be sure to check out the pétanque (lawn bowling) and chess areas at Luxembourg Gardens.
Date: Day 3
Location: Paris, France
Activities:
  • Visit Musée d'Orsay
  • Visit The Sainte-Chapelle
  • Visit Palais Garnier
Notes: The best time to visit the Sainte-Chapelle is in the morning from Tuesday to Friday.
🥐Don't forget to try the croissants at a local bakery.🥐
